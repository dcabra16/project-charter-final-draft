📋 1. Project Title & Description

IT Department Career Advancement Project

My mission is to gain the skills and experience needed to transition into an IT role at my current workplace, beginning with a Junior Systems Administrator or IT Support position. My vision is to continue developing within the company and grow into a Systems Administrator role within the next three to five years. The goal of my project is to build the technical expertise, professional relationships, and internal visibility needed to make that move possible. I will do this by completing relevant IT training and certifications, applying new skills in practical settings at work, and seeking mentorship from experienced IT professionals. I also aim to strengthen my communication and problem-solving skills to better support users and contribute to my organization’s IT operations.

📊 9. Project Methodology(PENDING)

I chose Hybrid—Iterative for my project.


Reasons:

  • My requirements are clear enough to plan but flexible enough to improve.
  • I can turn in drafts (bio, work samples, mockups) early and revise them after feedback.
  • Stakeholders (professor, peers, mentors) are available to review and give suggestions during the project.
  • It keeps me on schedule while still letting me make changes, which is better than just locking myself into one plan.

⚙️ 10. Project Tool Selection

I chose to use Asana for my project tool


Reasons:

  • Asana gives me more structure than Trello but isn't as overcomplicated as Monday.com
  • Since my project is Hybrid—Iterative, I need something that lets me plan phases, set deadlines, and still make adjustments when I get feedback.
  • Asana's timeline and dependency features make it easier for me to see what comes first, what depends on something else, and what can be worked on in parallel.
